From Vision to Reality: The Willkai Story

Founded in 2015, Willkai Window Co., Ltd. began with a simple mission: to elevate the standard of aluminum and glass construction in Vietnam. Our founders, a team of passionate engineers and construction experts, saw an opportunity to introduce innovative techniques and a client-centric approach to the market.

Over the years, we have grown from a small workshop into a trusted partner for the nation's leading architects, contractors, and investors, delivering precision, quality, and reliability on every project.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be the premier provider of innovative aluminum and glass solutions, exceeding client expectations through superior craftsmanship, cutting-edge technology, and a commitment to sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To shape the skylines of tomorrow, contributing to a built environment that is more beautiful, efficient, and resilient.
